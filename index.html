About

Unplugged Conf is a learning playground for software developpers and all people that like to discover things one step at a time. It's fun but seriously interesting!

The core idea is that we don't need slides or computers to share value and strong ideas. At Unplugged Conf when you present something it's just you and people around, you can use whatever you need to help you explaining things (glasses, chairs, people, whatever...), you can even use a pen and a paper board! Just, no computers allowed!

Ah, yes, we also forgot to mention, that an Unplugged conf should take place in a bar, because sharing knowledge can also be done in a convivial and funny way!

Time to relax

Agenda

But, how does it works?

The organisation of the conf will follow this program:

  • All talks are 5 min (more or less 1 or 2 min, we're cool people)
  • After a short introduction of our evening host, we'll have speakers talks for about 1h and something. These are all well known speakers, previously announced
  • 30 min break
  • during this time, we'll open a market place where people in the room can propose anything she/he wants to talk about. At the end, the comitee take all the submissions and setup the program.
  • then we'll start people's presentations
  • ...until it's done ;-)

As you have only 5 min, you'll have keep focused on one core idea and that's the way we learn better!

Unplugged Conf is really the best way to share and learn with fun and confidence in the comfort a friendly place. Drinking during talks is allowed too!

Prepare to be surprised!
